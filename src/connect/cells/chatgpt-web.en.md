# Connect ChatGPT in the browser to kumbuka

1. In ChatGPT go to **Plugins** in the navigation and click the **+** in the top right.
2. The **New Plugin** dialog opens.
   [shot 2: ChatGPT: New Plugin]
3. Enter the **Endpoint URL** under **Server URL**, choose **OAuth** for **Authentication** and tick **“I understand and want to continue”**.
   {{ENDPOINT}}
   [shot 3: ChatGPT: OAuth + confirmation]
4. Click **Create**. The connect dialog opens — click **“Sign in with Kumbuka”** there.
   [shot 4: ChatGPT: add Kumbuka]
5. Sign in to kumbuka and **grant** the requested access privileges.
   [shot 5a: Sign in to kumbuka]
   [shot 5b: Grant access]
6. Once granted, the connector is connected. Now follow the **Instruction for the assistant** below to set up your project.
   [shot 6: ChatGPT: connected]
