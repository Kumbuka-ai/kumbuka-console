# Connect Claude in the browser to kumbuka

1. In your browser open Claude.ai and in the “Customize” menu go to the Connectors management.
   [shot 1: Claude: Connectors management]
2. Create a new custom connector and enter the **Endpoint URL**. Then click **Add**.
   {{ENDPOINT}}
   [shot 2: Claude: endpoint field]
3. The connector shows “You are not connected to Kumbuka yet.” — click **Connect**.
   [shot 3: Claude: not connected yet]
4. Sign in to kumbuka and **grant** the requested access privileges.
   [shot 4a: Sign in to kumbuka]
   [shot 4b: Grant access]
5. The message **“Connected to Kumbuka.”** confirms completion. Now follow the **Instruction for the assistant** below to set up your project.
   [shot 5: Claude: connected to kumbuka]
